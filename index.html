ANIMAL COINS

WHAT ARE ANIMAL COINS?

Animal Coins are a set of 3005 non-fungible tokens with unique artwork that represent endangered species.

A percetange of every sale is sent trustlessly and on-chain to the World Wildlife Fond. This is written into each token's contract, ensuring its patronage in perpetuity.

GOAL

Animal Coin's goal is the continuation of each species for its own sake and the role it plays in the greater ecosystem. Animal Coins will stay rare, but the species they represent will become more populous.

The animals are from the IUCN's Red List of Critically Endangered Animals.
